Pick Types

Each fantasy player has a roster of Survivor castaways. How your picks score depends on their type:

TypeHow It Scores
Draft Full points. Your core picks selected during the draft.
Wildcard (½ pts) Earns half of whatever points the castaway accumulates. Chosen after the first episode (after the first elimination) from any remaining castaway. A bonus pick on top of your draft.
Replacement (½ pts) Post-merge replacement for an eliminated wildcard pick. Earns half points minus pre-merge tribal count ({{ season.num_players - season.left_at_jury if season else '?' }} tribals before merge).
Replacement Post-merge replacement for an eliminated draft pick. Earns full points minus pre-merge tribal count.

Win Probabilities

The leaderboard shows each fantasy player's estimated chance of winning the season.

This is calculated by simulating all possible elimination orders for the remaining castaways:

  • 8 or fewer remaining — all permutations are checked (exhaustive)
  • More than 8 remaining — 50,000 random orderings are sampled

For each simulated outcome, the full leaderboard is recalculated, and the fantasy player who would win is tallied. The win percentage is how often each player comes out on top.

Data Sources

All game data (boot order, tribe assignments, challenge results, advantages, confessionals) comes from the survivoR open-source dataset, maintained by the community. Player profiles link to survivorstatsdb.com.

Data is automatically refreshed daily at 8am EST.
